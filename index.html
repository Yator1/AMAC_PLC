AMAC Expands with Strategic Share Transfer!

By Admin | March 20, 2025

Africa Mega AgriCorp PLC (AMAC) is taking a bold step toward revolutionizing agribusiness! With approval from the Capital Markets Authority (CMA), AMAC has successfully transferred a significant stake of its issued shares to Gulf Africa Innovations Ltd (GAI) through a private transfer.

This marks a new era of innovation, market expansion, and value creation for investors and stakeholders. Leveraging GAI’s cutting-edge technology, AMAC is set to transform the agricultural supply chain. Our advanced Agritech platform will seamlessly connect farmers across Kenya and East Africa with buyers in both local and global markets.

"This partnership is more than just a share transfer—it’s a gateway to the future of agribusiness,"

With state-of-the-art cold storage facilities and a premier distribution hub at the Dubai Multi Commodities Centre (DMCC), we are ensuring faster market access, streamlined logistics, and instant farmer payments through our trusted banking partners.
